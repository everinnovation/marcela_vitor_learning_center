Who We Are

Marcela Vitor Learning Center is a vibrant, bilingual space where children grow, explore, and thrive. Our approach blends Montessori principles with a warm, multicultural environment.

🎯 Our Mission

We nurture each child's curiosity and individuality, fostering growth through play, exploration, and meaningful relationships. Our bilingual curriculum (Portuguese-English) encourages cultural awareness and a lifelong love of learning.

🌟 Our Vision

To inspire confident, compassionate learners who celebrate diversity and contribute positively to their communities.

👩‍🏫 Meet Our Director

Marcela Vitor is a speech-language pathologist with 9 years of experience in Massachusetts schools. Her passion for education and child development shapes our center's caring, inclusive philosophy.

Our team of educators brings expertise, warmth, and dedication to every child’s journey.

💡 Our Core Values

We believe in respect, empathy, diversity, and the joy of learning. Our programs blend quality education and language development for children from 12 months to preschool.

🎨 Creativity

We encourage creative expression through music, art, and imaginative play.

🤝 Teamwork

Children learn to collaborate, share, and build friendships through group activities and sports.

🌍 Diversity

We celebrate cultural traditions and embrace diversity in joyful and meaningful ways.
